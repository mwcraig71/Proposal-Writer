How These Settings Work

  • The selected AI provider and model are used for all AI features across the application
  • Writing preferences apply to all AI rewrites and can be supplemented with per-task instructions
  • The proposal review prompts define how the AI evaluates uploaded proposals for compliance and quality
  • Switching providers does not affect your existing data
