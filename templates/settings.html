Template Customization Tips

  • Keep the placeholder text (project title, owner info, etc.) in the template - these get replaced with actual project data
  • You can adjust formatting, fonts, spacing, and layout as needed
  • The system looks for specific text patterns to replace - avoid changing the placeholder content itself
  • Test your custom template by downloading a project after importing
